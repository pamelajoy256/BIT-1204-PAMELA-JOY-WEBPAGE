Background

Mountains of the University is a public University located in Fort Portal , Western Uganda. The University was established in 2005 to provide quality education and research to support communityu development and sustainable development . It became a public University in 2022.

The University focuses on teaching , research community engagement to transform society through Education , Innovation and Environmental conservation.
